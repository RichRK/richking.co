Privacy

Here are a few quick notes on how I protect your privacy when you use this site:

  • I don't use any analytic products on 'Remember When' to track who you are or what you're doing. I trust myself to have created a site that you'll enjoy using without needing to track every action you take on here.
  • I use Vimeo to display the videos you see in the Portfolio section. They place cookies on your device when you choose to watch a video, but solely Vimeo ones — no other third-parties are involved.
  • Any information you send to me—via the Bookings or Contact sections, for example—I use solely for the purpose of communicating with you regarding your interest in 'Remember When'.
  • The only third-party that your information gets shared with is my website host, Netlifylaunch, and only for storage purposes, i.e. similar to how services like Dropbox and Google Drive store data on people's behalf. Other than that, none of the information you provide me is shared with third-parties.
  • I don't use any automated email services — if you receive a message from me, it's one I've personally written and sent.

Any thoughts or concerns? Let me know!
